# Privacy Policy

**ZenSON JSON Prettifier – Browser Extension**
Last updated: March 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the **ZenSON JSON Prettifier** Opera browser extension ("the Extension", "we", "our", or "us") handles user information.

The Extension is designed to format and prettify JSON content directly within your browser for improved readability.

## 2. Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Specifically:

* We do not collect personal information.
* We do not track browsing activity.
* We do not use analytics tools.
* We do not use cookies.
* We do not transmit any data to external servers.

## 3. How the Extension Works

The Extension:

* Detects JSON content loaded in your browser.
* Formats (prettifies) the JSON locally within your browser.
* Does not send JSON data or any other content outside your device.

All processing occurs locally on the user's device.

## 4. Data Storage

The Extension:

* Does not store user data remotely.
* Does not maintain external databases.

## 5. Permissions

The Extension may request certain browser permissions solely to:

* Access page content to detect and format JSON.
* Modify the display of JSON content within the browser.

These permissions are used strictly for the functionality of the Extension and not for data collection.

## 6. Third-Party Services

The Extension:

* Does not use third-party services.
* Does not integrate advertising.
* Does not embed external trackers.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect or process personal data from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
